Card Reading

Angelic / Oracle / Tarot Reading

Mainly used for General reading, Relationship/Marriage, Health, Spirituality, Job/Business/Finance. These are all forms of divination using cards:
Tarot Cards: A deck of 78 cards with specific symbolism and meanings. I interpret the cards to provide insights into past, present, and future events.
Oracle Cards: These decks with a variety of themes and messages. They are often more intuitive and less structured than tarot cards which provide messages from Universe.
Angelic Cards: A type of oracle reading that focuses on messages from angels or spirit guides.
