Sand Reading

Sand Reading is a form of fortune telling that involves interpretation of lines in sand to gain insights and guidance. I scatter sand on a surface and then read the resulting shapes and lines with my intuition.

The flowing nature of sand allows for a unique reading that captures the ever-changing energies of your life situation.

  • Reveals patterns and energies in your life
  • Provides insights into current life flow
  • Helps identify obstacles and opportunities

Candle Wax Reading

Candle Wax Reading is an ancient form of divination where I ask a question and light a candle, and allow it to burn down, then interpret the resulting wax drippings and formations to gain insight into emotions, events, or energies.

This reading is particularly effective for understanding the energies surrounding specific situations or questions.

  • Interprets wax patterns for spiritual messages
  • Provides clarity on specific questions
  • Reveals hidden influences and energies

Dowsing

Pendulum techniques are used for seeking answers or information by interpreting the movements of a suspended pendulum. Common uses include divination, finding lost objects, healing, and yes-no answers. The pendulum acts as a tool to connect with the subconscious mind or a higher source of wisdom, with its movements believed to channel the user's intentions or subtle energies.

Dowsing can be used for a variety of purposes including finding lost objects, identifying energy blockages, and receiving yes/no answers to questions.

  • Accesses subconscious knowledge through pendulum movement
  • Provides clear yes/no answers to specific questions
  • Helps identify energy blockages and imbalances
