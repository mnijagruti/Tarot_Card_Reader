About Rashmi

Guiding you with Tarot, Reiki, and Spiritual Healing for over 12 years.

Atma Namaste 🙏

I’m Rashmi, a Certified 🌻 Tarot Reader, 🌻 Reiki Master, and 🌻 Lama Fera Healer with over 12 years of experience. As a clairvoyant, my passion lies in helping individuals uncover their true potential and lead more fulfilling lives.

My spiritual journey began at a young age, and over the years I’ve immersed myself in various modalities to deepen my understanding of the mind-body-spirit connection. This extensive experience has enabled me to craft a personalized healing approach for each of my clients.

🔮 Tarot Reading

I use Tarot as a tool for insight, reflection, and self-discovery, helping you understand your current situation, recognize the energies influencing your life, and make informed decisions for your future. Every reading is approached with compassion, honesty, and respect for your unique journey, ensuring confidentiality and trust.

🌟 Reiki Healing

As a Certified Reiki Grandmaster, I channel healing energy to restore balance, relaxation, and well-being. My approach combines traditional Reiki techniques with intuitive healing to release negative energy, emotional blockages, and support your body’s natural healing processes.

I believe in the unique power of every individual to shape their reality. That's why I offer tailored, personalized services that address your specific needs and preferences. Virtual consultations are available for your convenience. Whether you seek guidance, healing, or self-discovery, I’m here to support you on your transformative journey.

Thank you for considering my services. I look forward to helping you achieve greater balance, fulfilment, and overall well-being.
