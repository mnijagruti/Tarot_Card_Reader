Course Description

Tarot card reading is a practice that uses tarot cards to gain insights into the past, present, or future events. A deck of 78 cards with specific symbolism and meanings. By formulating a question and interpreting the drawn cards, you can uncover valuable information about your life.

This form of cartomancy (fortune-telling or divination using cards) serves as a tool for spiritual guidance, helping you connect with your inner wisdom.

What Can Tarot Cards Be Used For?

Tarot cards provide guidance on various aspects of your life, such as:

  • Love and relationships
  • Money and finances
  • Career and goals
  • General life path

Rather than predicting the future, tarot readings offer insights and clarity, helping you understand what you need to know about a particular situation. They reflect your current path and allow you to make informed decisions.

How Do Tarot Cards Work?

During a tarot reading, your energy influences the selection of the cards. The cards serve as a mirror, reflecting your life and circumstances. The reader's wisdom, knowledge, and experience are essential in interpreting the deeper truths within each card. Remember, the future is fluid, and your actions can change the course of your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What questions can I ask tarot cards?

You can ask tarot cards anything, from specific issues to broader life questions. There are no limits to the types of questions tarot can help you resolve and understand. The answers are already out there, waiting for you to access them.

Do I need any prior experience with tarot?

No prior experience is needed. This course is designed for beginners and will take you from basic concepts to advanced tarot reading techniques.

What materials do I need for the course?

You will need a standard Rider-Waite tarot deck or any deck you feel connected to. All other learning materials will be provided during the course.
